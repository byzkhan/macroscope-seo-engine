# SEO Rules

## Keyword Placement
- Primary keyword must appear in: H1 title, first 100 words, meta description, URL slug
- Primary keyword should appear in at least 2 H2 headings (naturally, not forced)
- Secondary keywords distributed across H2 headings and body text
- No keyword stuffing — density target: 0.5-2.5%

## Title Tag
- Maximum 60 characters (Google truncates after ~60)
- Front-load primary keyword
- Include a value proposition or differentiator
- No pipes or brackets — use colons or dashes if needed

## Meta Description
- 120-155 characters (Google truncates after ~155)
- Include primary keyword naturally
- Write as a compelling summary, not keyword soup
- Include a call to action or value statement

## URL Slug
- Lowercase, hyphen-separated
- Include primary keyword
- Keep under 60 characters
- No stop words unless needed for readability

## Heading Hierarchy
- Exactly one H1 per page (the title)
- H2s for major sections (4-7 per article)
- H3s for subsections under H2s only
- Never skip levels (H1 → H3 without H2)
- Include keywords in headings naturally

## Internal Linking
- Minimum 3 internal links per article
- Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")
- Link to related blog posts, product pages, and pillar content
- Distribute links throughout the article, not clustered

## Content Depth
- Minimum 1,500 words for ranking potential
- Cover the topic comprehensively — anticipate follow-up questions
- Include data, examples, and actionable takeaways
- Update published content when new information is available
